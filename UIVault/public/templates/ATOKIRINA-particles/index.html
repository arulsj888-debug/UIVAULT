Woodsprites - Seeds of the Sacred Tree

ATOKIRINA

Atokirina' are seeds of the Tree of Souls (Vitraya Ramunong), pure and sacred spirits in Na'vi culture. These jellyfish-like woodsprites carry deep spiritual significance, often appearing as omens or guiding signs from Eywa.
